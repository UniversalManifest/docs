# Metaverse Portaling and Digital-Worlds Content Pack

**Date:** 2026-04-22  
**Status:** Completion report for `WO-0199`

## Purpose

Package the lead public content lane for metaverse portaling, avatars, and portable content/policy across digital worlds.

## Core public framing

Universal Manifest helps a subject move between digital worlds without losing the portable context that the destination world is allowed to project.

That portable context can include:

- identity references
- avatar and profile settings
- content pointers
- portability permissions
- presentation or display policies
- proof-bearing claims where needed

The destination world does not have to ingest an entire world-state database. It only needs one portable document it can understand and one ruleset for what it may project.

## Explorer payload

The lead explorer lane should frame:

- the jump between worlds,
- the avatar/profile continuity,
- the content-pointer continuity,
- and the fact that consent/policy travels too.

## Audience payload

The metaverse audience page should focus on:

- portaling
- avatars
- interoperable world entry
- content portability
- spatial/contextual policy projection
