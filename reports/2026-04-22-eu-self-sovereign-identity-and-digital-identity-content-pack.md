# EU Self-Sovereign Identity and Digital-Identity Content Pack

**Date:** 2026-04-22  
**Status:** Completion report for `WO-0200`

## Purpose

Package the audience-facing content for EU self-sovereign identity and related digital-identity ecosystems.

## Core audience framing

For this audience, Universal Manifest should not be framed as a replacement for wallets, credentials, or identity standards.

It should be framed as:

- a portable envelope,
- a way to carry identity-adjacent state,
- a way to combine claims, consent, pointers, and policy in one bounded document,
- a way to complement credential and identity ecosystems without forcing one storage or trust substrate.

## Audience page focus

The EU SSI page should emphasize:

- standards complementarity
- pointer-first, non-monolithic architecture
- claims plus consent plus portable state
- separation between normative spec and ecosystem-specific deployments
